Immediate education points

Referral protocol directory

Edit these pathways according to your local QMH/AED workflow. This app gives a quick structured page so you can keep referral routes in one place.

Geriatric specialist / CGAT

Consider when frailty, repeated falls, functional decline, caregiver burnout, recurrent AED attendance, or unsafe discharge is identified.

Trigger: frail elder with multiple geriatric syndromes.
Prepare: baseline mobility, cognition, ADL support, home situation, recent admissions.
Outcome: specialist review, ward referral, outpatient geriatric follow-up, case management advice.

Physiotherapy / OT

Consider for gait instability, transfer problem, deconditioning, equipment need, home barrier concern.

Trigger: unsafe ambulation or recent decline from baseline.
Prepare: walking aid use, stair ability, pain, balance, carer assistance level.
Outcome: mobility training, transfer advice, aids, home modification suggestion.

Medical social worker

Consider when there is poor social support, financial stress, abuse concern, institutional placement issue, carer breakdown.

Trigger: lives alone, neglect risk, no escort, crisis at home.
Prepare: household members, income support issue, safeguarding concern, discharge barrier.
Outcome: crisis intervention, community resource linkage, placement planning, financial guidance.

Community NGO / outreach

Consider for education, home support, escort, meal, exercise, caregiver support, dementia support.

Trigger: mild-to-moderate support need, suitable for community follow-up.
Prepare: district, language, mobility status, phone contact, caregiver details.
Outcome: community program, day care, home visit, education reinforcement.
