Referral protocols
Fast Track GDH Advancing SOPD QMH Clinic FU PGT CGAT CVMO ICDS / IDSP PSCC CNS GOPC Palliative
1. Fast Track Clinic in FYKH
Consult Geriatrician for need of Fast Track Clinic in FYKH.
Contact FYKH GDH nurse for appointment, Tel: 2855 6177.
Fax referral form endorsed by Geriatrician to GDH, Fax: 2855 6169.
After confirmation, inform patient of appointment and transport arrangement.
2. Geriatric Day Hospital (FYKH / TWH)
Consult Geriatrician for GDH need.
FYKH GDH Tel: 2855 6177; TWH GDH Tel: 2589 8178.
Fax referral to FYKH GDH 2855 6169 or TWH GDH 2546 5510.
Inform patient once appointment is confirmed.
3. Advancing SOPD appointment
Consult Geriatrician for advancing SOPD requirement.
Fax referral plus doctor memo to QMH / GH / TWH as appropriate.
For memory / fall / nutritional clinic in QMH, contact geriatric nurse directly.
After booking, notify patient by phone.
4. Early follow-up in QMH Geriatric Clinics
Fax endorsed referral to QMH Medicine S6.
Aim for new Geriatric appointment within 2 weeks when appropriate.
QMH Medicine S6 Fax: 2255 3018; Tel: 2255 3375 / 2255 1242.
5. Referral to Psychogeriatric Outreach Service Team (PGT)
For psychogeriatric intervention in RCHE, consult Geriatrician.
Contact APN in outreach service team.
Fax conjoint care plan to 2559 0217.
Provide phone handover to receiving APN.
6. Referral to CGAT
For early discharge support in RCHE, contact FYKH CGAT office 2855 6144.
Fax conjoint care plan to 2872 4132.
Give brief clinical handover to APN.
7. Referral to CVMO (RCHE CGAT old cases only)
Consult Geriatrician for post-discharge follow-up interventions.
Contact CGAT office and fax conjoint care plan.
Coordinate with respective APN for action.
8. New case / active case to ICDS / IDSP
Identify new or active status after CGA.
For new cases, fax discharge recommendation and referral documents.
ICDS office Tel: 2255 6743; Fax: 2986 3192.
Give handover to case manager or IDSP nurse manager.
9. PSCC support / referral
Verify HRE status in CMS.
Contact PSCC hotline if further handover is needed.
PSCC hotline Tel: 2996 2333.
10. Referral to CNS
Consult Geriatrician for community nursing need.
For new case, send referral and conjoint care plan.
HKWC CNS liaison Tel: 2589 2300; Fax: 2540 4899.
11. GOPC appointment
Use IVAS booking first.
If quota full, contact GOPC nurse in charge for force-in quota.
Notify patient of appointment and transport arrangement.
12. Palliative home care services
Consult Geriatrician for early discharge and follow-up action.
Contact NC (Palliative) and fax care plan when needed.
GH Palliative Office Fax: 2873 2767.
